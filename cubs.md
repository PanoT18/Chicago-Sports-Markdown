# Chicago Cubs

![Cubs Logo](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/8/80/Chicago_Cubs_logo.svg)

The Cubs are one of the most well known MLB franchises and had one of the longest droughts for a major city World Series.

---

## Highlights

- Stadium: **Wrigley Field**
- Biggest Moment: **Breaking the 108 year drought in 2016**
- Famous Tradition: **Flying the W flag after wins**

---

### Legendary Cubs

- Ernie Banks
- Sammy Sosa
- Kris Bryant
- Anthony Rizzo
